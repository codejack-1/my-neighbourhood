There are a few symptoms that mean you might need a root canal—

  • Severe pain while chewing or biting
  • Pimples on the gums
  • A chipped or cracked tooth
  • Lingering sensitivity to hot or cold, even after the sensation has been removed
  • Swollen or tender gums
  • Deep decay or darkening of the gums.
Procedure:

A root canal is performed when the root canal specialist removes the infected pulp and nerve in the root of the tooth cleans and shapes the inside of the root

Laser is a fast and effective method that uses heat and light to perform a wide variety of dental procedures. Most importantly, laser dentistry is virtually PAIN-FREE! The dentist uses a laser to access an infected area in the mouth, to kill the infected tissue and bacteria. We use dental lasers for a variety of procedures to provide painless dental treatment to the patient. We use lasers extensively for gum surgeries that have shown amazing results and benefits and faster healing in patients post-surgery.

BENIFITS OF DENTAL LASER:
  • A faster healing time.
  • Less post-surgical bleeding.
  • Less pain.
  • Anesthesia may not be necessary.
  • Lasers are sterile, which means there is less chance for an infection.
  • Lasers are extremely precise, so less healthy tissue has to be removed.

Smile makeover/designing is a way to invest in yourself by investing in your smile. It improves your smile by the whitening, brightening, and straightening your teeth. Why not invest in your smile?

A smile makeover is a series of dental procedures that you undergo primarily for cosmetic purposes. For patients with healthy, but misaligned or gapped teeth, a smile makeover can transform a smile into a more aesthetically pleasing one, often boosting confidence and making patients want to show off their pearly whites more frequently. In some cases, our in-house dermatologist is also consulted to give your skin and face a complete makeover using Botox and other techniques.

Dr. Sapna will assess your case using 3D scans of the mouth, x-rays, and other tools in order to plan your smile design. We believe in a conservative approach. So that minimal and only the required treatment is done to get the desired results and nothing more. This makes our treatment protocol world class and cost effective for the patient.

What is Included in a Smile Makeover?

A smile makeover combines a variety of cosmetic dental procedures, including:

Teeth whitening, to brighten up discolored teeth;

Porcelain veneers, which are thin, shell-like, layers of porcelain that are placed over existing teeth that have flaws, such as chips or discolored spots.

Braces or Invisalign, which can be used to correct misaligned teeth; and Crowns, while typically a functional procedure, can also be used to cover teeth that have unattractive flaws.

Invisalign clear aligners are made from SmartTrack material, so they are more comfortable and easier to put on and take off. They are transparent and aesthetically pleasing.

Dentures are removable appliances that can replace missing teeth and help restore your smile. If you’ve lost all of your natural teeth or few of them, whether from gum disease, tooth decay, or injury, replacing missing teeth will benefit your appearance and your health. That’s because dentures make it easier to eat and speak better than you could without teeth—things that people often take for granted.

We offer different types of dentures ranging from flexible snap-on dentures to partial dentures. These dentures offer maximum comfort and help in giving shape to the face and cheek muscles that sag due to missing teeth. You will see that dentures make you look younger and make your excess wrinkles disappear.
